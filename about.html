About Us

The Velvet Grape Lounge was born out of a shared obsession among visionaries. What began as an exclusive circle of partners funding a singular vision has evolved into a premier destination for subterranean refinement. Our founders established this space to bypass conventional commercial limits, pouring their collective resources directly into acquiring unlisted allocations, pristine cellar vaults, and preserving the deep, sacred history of the vine.

Our Inspiration

The Grand-Dame of Champagne

Veuve Clicquot

Our philosophy draws deep inspiration from Barbe-Nicole Clicquot—the legendary "Grande Dame of Champagne." Widowed at just 27, she defied rigid 19th-century societal conventions to take control of her late husband's wine business, single-handedly steering the house through economic ruin and fierce wartime blockades.

Madame Clicquot entirely revolutionized the industry by inventing the riddling table mechanism—a breakthrough technique that cleanly removed sediment from bottles, transforming champagne into the crystal-clear luxury asset enjoyed by global high society today.

Her audacious spirit and unrelenting commitment to perfection define the very DNA of our unlisted subterranean collection. We operate under her timeless guiding principle: "Only one quality, the finest."
