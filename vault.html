Welcome to the Vault

A sweet escape for the patrons of The Velvet Grape Lounge. This digital sanctuary unseals our most closely guarded vintages, limited-allocation imports, and unlisted cellar estates. It is a live threshold to the extraordinary—designed for those who do not just drink wine, but accumulate experiences.

The VIP Experience

01 / Elite Gatherings

Wine O'Clock À Minuit

Every Thursday at the stroke of midnight, a single unlisted table of six is opened for a blind tasting of ancestral vintages valued over $1,000, hosted personally by our Master Sommelier. Only Vault profiles hold access keys to secure these seats.

02 / Bespoke Cellaring

Private Allocation

Configure your unique sensory blueprint profile. Our procurement team will directly intercept and hold back high-demand allocations from fine European wine houses, cellaring them under your name until your next lounge appearance.
