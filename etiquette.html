House Etiquette

To preserve the intimate atmosphere and unlisted sanctity of The Vault, all patrons are required to honor our house protocols.

01 / The Aesthetic

Avant-Garde Attire

We enforce a strict dark-luxe or semi-formal dress code. Architectural silhouettes, tailored suiting, and evening attire are celebrated. Casual sportswear, graphics, and open-toed footwear will be discreetly turned away at the threshold to maintain our refined sensory environment.

02 / The Atmosphere

Digital Discretion

To protect the absolute privacy of our curators and guests, flash photography and extended video recording are strictly prohibited inside the subterranean chambers. We kindly request all communication devices remain muted throughout your allocation.

03 / The Arrival

Temporal Precision

Cellar allocations are synchronized with our sommeliers' rotational pouring schedules. Due to the high-demand nature of our finite seating arrangements, reservations are held for exactly 15 minutes before being forfeit to waitlisted patrons.
