Our Specialties

01 / Excellence Democratized

The Salon Lounge

Our main Salon Lounge balances an approachable yet sophisticated atmosphere with a curated menu of exceptional regional harvests and master-vintner classics. Built for enthusiasts and connoisseurs alike, we offer world-class pours and pairingsdesigned to accomodate anyone who honors the craft, making premium wine a shared hobby rather than an exclusive indulgence.

02 / Bespoke Cellaring

Private Allocation

Featuring vintage wines sourced from the finest vineyards all around the world, partnering with popular wine estates, our procurement team will directly intercept and hold back high-demand allocations from fine European wine houses, cellaring them under your name until your next lounge appearance with a premium fee of $100.

03 / Events & Wine Tastings

Wine O'Clock À Minuit

Every Thursday midnight, we have a dedicated open-house event built to quench your raw curiosity and fester your deep appreciation for wine. From 6:00PM until 6:00 AM, we offer our entire elite lounge experience, full sommelier-guided tasting flights of our bestsellers, and a detailed background of the wine from grape to bottle. During the event, we offer a 50% discount all with the goal to encourage wine appreciation at affordable prices.
